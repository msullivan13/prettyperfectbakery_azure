About Pretty Perfect Bakery

Welcome to Pretty Perfect Bakery, where every treat is baked with love and a touch of creativity! Nestled in the heart of Lakeland, Pretty Perfect began with a simple idea: to bring joy to the community through delicious, handcrafted treats. What started as a passion for baking in our home kitchen has blossomed into a beloved neighborhood bakery, serving up everything from classic cupcakes to exquisite custom cakes.

Our Backstory

The Pretty Perfect Bakery began in 2004 when our two founders traveled overseas for a job opportunity, driven by their love for smiles, they decided to open a bakery in Lakeland, Florida. From then, their business has expanded to include neighboring cities. What started as a little shop in South Lakeland, has grown into three locations, with the goal of more across Florida and into other states to share their passion for delicious treats.

Our Values

At The Pretty Perfect Bakery, we value:

Quality – We’re committed to serving the best-baked goods America has ever seen. We use ethically sourced ingredients and locally made love.

Community – We hope to create a welcoming space where everyone can connect, relax, and feel happy.

Sustainability – We focus on sustainable practices and collaborate with local businesses to foster a healthier planet and a more vibrant community.

Meet the Team

Our team is made up of skilled bakers, decorators, and pastry chefs, each bringing their unique talent and passion to the kitchen. Emma and Jack lead with a hands-on approach, making sure every treat that leaves our doors lives up to our standards of quality and care.

Our Promise

At Pretty Perfect Bakery, we’re more than just a bakery – we’re a place where memories are made, and celebrations come to life. We look forward to welcoming you into our cozy space and helping make your sweetest moments unforgettable.
